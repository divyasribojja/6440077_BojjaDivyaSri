Community News Bulletin

The city council has announced several new initiatives aimed at improving community engagement and local services. These programs will launch over the next six months.

The first initiative is a neighborhood beautification project that will provide grants to residents who want to improve their streets with planters, murals, or other aesthetic enhancements.

Second, the council is launching a new digital platform for reporting local issues like potholes, broken streetlights, or other maintenance needs. This system promises faster response times.

Finally, the community center will extend its hours and offer new programs for seniors and youth, including technology classes and fitness programs.
